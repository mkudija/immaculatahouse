About Immaculata House

Mission: In the charism of communal life with consecrated religious sisters, we will inspire the young women to discern and develop their distinctively feminine God given vocation and individual interests and talents through internalized Catholic virtues.

Vision: To foster saints among college-aged women who uniquely fulfill civic & family obligations within Catholic moral principles & Gospel values to become leaders in their communities.



Immaculata House is a unique, woman-only community that we desire to make a second home for our residents. We foster a community of spiritual nourishment, including our chapel which is available 24 hours a day.
